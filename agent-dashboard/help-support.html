Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Go to your Settings and click on 'Change Password'. Follow the instructions to reset your password.
Where can I view my submitted listings?
Navigate to the 'My Listings' section in your dashboard to view all your submitted properties.
How do I contact support?
For support, please return to the homepage and scroll to the footer, where you'll find our contact email and social media links for assistance.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to your profile page and click 'Edit Profile' to update your information.
